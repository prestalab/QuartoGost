## Norm-control checklist for a study guide

- Is the edition type explicitly identified as `учебное пособие` or the correct educational subtype?
- If there is a recommendation or approval, is it written in the front matter exactly as provided by the institution?
- Does the title page contain the key publishing fields needed by the local publisher or university?
- Is the target audience or course level stated clearly?
- Does the structure include both explanatory material and learning apparatus such as examples, exercises, or control questions?
- Are references and appendices present when the material requires them?
- If the guide is intended for formal publication, were local ISBN, reviewer, annotation, and publication-block requirements checked separately?
- Are the local formatting parameters respected: 1.9/1.9/1.9/2.4 cm margins, 1.5 cm footer distance, TNR 16 pt, single spacing, justified text?
- Does each chapter start on a new page and end with reinforcement material?
- Are heading levels visually consistent, with level 1 in uppercase and lower levels in lowercase?
- Are formulas, tables, and illustrations numbered consistently and placed after textual references?
