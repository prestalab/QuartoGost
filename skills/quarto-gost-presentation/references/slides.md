## Suggested slide flow

- title
- relevance and problem statement
- goal and tasks
- architecture or methodology
- key figures or tables
- results
- conclusions
- reserve slides

## Style guidance

- short headings
- limited bullet count
- readable charts
- consistent terminology with the written report or dissertation
- use the `presentation` plotting profile from `scripts\julia\plotting.jl`
- keep presentation charts colorized; do not manually force grayscale here
- avoid hardcoded plot colors unless the slide deck has an approved palette
