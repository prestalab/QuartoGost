## Norm-control checklist for ESPD-style text documents

- Are the required service sheets present in the Word reference file?
- Are section, subsection, point, and subpoint numbers hierarchical and dot-separated?
- Are section numbers written without a trailing dot?
- Are enumerations formatted consistently with dashes or lettered items?
- If signatures or approvals are required, are they kept in the title or approval sheets rather than typed into the main body?
- If change tracking is required by the document type, is the change-registration sheet preserved?
- If the document is split into parts or books, are designation and naming consistent across all parts?
