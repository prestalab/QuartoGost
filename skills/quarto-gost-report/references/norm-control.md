## Norm-control checklist for GOST 7.32 reports

- Is the title sheet complete and aligned with the chosen report type?
- Is the list of executors present, unless the work has a single executor and the data was moved to the title sheet?
- Does the abstract include counts, keywords, and the core sequence of content blocks?
- Does the contents include all named sections, conclusion, sources, and appendices with page numbers?
- Are page margins, paragraph indent, font size, and line spacing aligned with the standard or local template?
- Is page numbering continuous, with the title page counted but not numbered visibly?
- Are all figures, tables, and formulas referenced from the text?
- Do formula explanations start with `где` and follow the formula immediately?
- Are appendices lettered and listed in the contents?
